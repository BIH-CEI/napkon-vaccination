Resource Profile: COVID-19 Encounter

Defining URL:https://www.napkon.de/fhir/StructureDefinition/covid19-encounter
Version:1.0.0
Name:COVID19Encounter
Title:COVID-19 Encounter
Status:Active as of 2021-10-05
Definition:

Whether a patient underwent medical treatment for COVID-19

Publisher:Charité - Universitätsmedizin Berlin
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

https://www.napkon.de/fhir/StructureDefinition/covid19-encounter

Guidance

This Encounter profile is used in conjunction with the SARS CoV2 Infection profile and COVID-19 Oxygen Therapy profile to encode the following information and response options.

Parameter Case Report Form Response Options
What was the course of the infection? I did not fall ill (had no symptoms) /
I was ill and at home /
I was ill and in hospital without oxygen therapy /
I was ill and in hospital with oxygen therapy /
I was ill and in hospital in an intensive care unit

The response options are encoded as follows using:

  • COVID-19 Encounter to specify the class of a medical encounter (home, inpatient, intensive care),
  • SARS CoV2 Infection to specify the actual infection, which is referenced in Encounter.diagnosis.condition,
  • COVID-19 Oxygen Therapy to indicate an oxygen therapy, if performed.
Response Option COVID19Encounter instance COVID19Encounter.class SARSCoV2Infection.verificationStatus COVID19OxygenTherapy instance
I did not fall ill (had no symptoms) ❌ - #refuted or #confirmed ❌
I was ill and at home ✅ #HH "home health" #confirmed ❌
I was ill and in hospital without oxygen therapy ✅ #IMP "inpatient encounter" #confirmed ❌
I was ill and in hospital with oxygen therapy ✅ #IMP "inpatient encounter" #confirmed ✅
I was ill and in hospital in an intensive care unit ✅ #intensivstationaer #confirmed ✅

This profile of a FHIR Encounter is derived from the KontaktGesundheitseinrichtung profile of the Medical Informatics Initiative (MII).
